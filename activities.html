Activities

5-A-Side Football

We provide 5-a-side football everyday as its the most popular sport in the world and in our gym. Learn from a professional coach every Monday at no additional cost to improve your game.

Indoor Volleyball

Every Monday and Friday we host volleyball games which have huge benefit for our members such as improving your aerobic ability, building coordination, balance and speed and it helps with your mental state.

Dance Fitness

At Fun Fitness we provide a exercise class everyday that brings together many different types of dance. Dance Fitness helps with stress relief and can help boost your confidence.

Boxing

We now have started to provide boxing classes on Tuesday and Thursday as it has become our most request demand from our members. There are many benefits to taking up boxing from helping with fat burning, increase your strength and power to help relieving stress and improving your co-ordination and body awareness.

PT Sessions

Our personal trainers provide one on one sessions to help find the best way to benefit you whether that is to lose weight, build muscle or just try and get fit. You will see results sooner and won’t suffer any injuries as you will be guided by a professional. Pop in to the gym and speak with one of our trainers for more info on what’s best for you.
